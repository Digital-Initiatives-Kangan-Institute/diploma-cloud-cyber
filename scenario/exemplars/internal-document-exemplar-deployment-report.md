> **Design intent for the deployment report exemplar specifically:**
>
> - Subject of the past engagement: a **different** workload from YAT-LMS (e.g. a small Australian business's customer-relationship system, or an order-management system) — students should see what good looks like *without* having a template they can shortcut by copying
> - Same shape as the Deployment Report template students will be filling in for AT2 (see `template-deployment-report-S1-CL1-AT2.md` once authored) — every section populated, every appendix represented
> - Plausible, realistic technical detail (real-looking AWS configurations, real-looking screenshots, real-looking IAM policies)
> - Authored by "MTS" for a fictional past client — establishes MTS as a credible consultancy
> - **Length and depth:** comparable to a real consulting deployment report — long enough to be useful as a reference, short enough that students can engage with it as a model rather than be intimidated
